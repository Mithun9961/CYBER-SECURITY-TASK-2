## Objective
To analyze a phishing email sample and identify common phishing characteristics such as brand impersonation, spoofed links, and social engineering tactics.

## Evidence Used
- Screenshot of a phishing email impersonating Apple Support

## Analysis Performed
- Identified brand knockoff impersonating Apple
- Observed generic greeting ("Hi {fname}")
- Noted presence of clickable links leading to non-Apple domains
- Identified fake support case reference
- Detected social engineering through curiosity-based wording
- Confirmed absence of official sender verification

## Conclusion
